About EcoTrack

EcoTrack is a web-based Carbon Footprint Tracker designed to help individuals understand and reduce their environmental impact. It calculates the amount of carbon dioxide (CO₂) emissions produced from daily activities such as travel, electricity usage, and food consumption.

what is a Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases released into the atmosphere as a result of human activities. These gases contribute to global warming and climate change. Everyday actions like driving vehicles, using electricity, and consuming certain foods all add to our carbon footprint.

How EcoTrack Works

  • Users enter their daily activities such as travel distance, electricity usage, and food habits.
  • The system calculates carbon emissions using predefined emission factors.
  • Results are displayed instantly with total CO₂ emissions.
  • Users can save their data and track their progress over time.

Objectives of the Project

  • To raise awareness about environmental sustainability.
  • To help users monitor and reduce their carbon footprint.
  • To provide a simple and interactive platform for eco-friendly decision-making.

Features

  • Real-time carbon footprint calculation
  • User login and data storage using PHP and MySQL
  • Dashboard to view past records
  • Responsive and user-friendly interface

Technologies Used

  • HTML & CSS for structure and design
  • JavaScript for dynamic calculations
  • PHP for backend processing
  • MySQL for database management

Why This Project Matters

Climate change is one of the biggest challenges facing our planet today. By tracking and reducing carbon emissions, individuals can contribute to a more sustainable future. EcoTrack encourages users to make environmentally conscious choices in their daily lives.
